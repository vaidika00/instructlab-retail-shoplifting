Knowledge leaf: Retail shrink as % of revenue
Taxonomy path: retail_shoplifting/economic_impact/shrink_as_percent_of_revenue

Primary author:
- vaidika

Description:
This knowledge leaf provides conceptual and quantitative explanations of
retail shrink, with a focus on how to calculate shrink as a percentage of
revenue and how different shrink levels affect gross margin and profit.
All examples and narratives are original work created for the purpose of
fine-tuning an industry-focused language model.

Upstream references (background reading, paraphrased; no verbatim text used):
- Public educational and industry materials explaining retail shrink,
  shrink calculation formulas, and typical shrink ranges.
- Openly available articles and whitepapers on causes of shrink
  (external theft, internal theft, process errors) and their business impact.
- General finance and accounting references on gross margin and net margin.
