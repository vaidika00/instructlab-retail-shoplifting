Knowledge leaf: National & global shoplifting trends
Taxonomy path: retail/shoplifting_industry/industry_trends_market_dynamics/national_global_shoplifting_trends

Primary author:

Purpose:
This knowledge leaf provides high-level explanations and synthetic,
scenario-based examples of national and global shoplifting trends. It
covers how shoplifting is measured over time, how trends differ across
countries and regions, and how analysts should interpret reported
changes in incident levels.

Origin of content:
All questions and answers in the associated qna.yaml file are original
work created by the author for this project. The examples, numbers, and
scenarios are illustrative and synthetic; they do not represent any
actual company’s internal data or confidential information.

Background sources consulted (paraphrased only):
- Publicly available industry reports and articles on national and
  international shoplifting and retail theft trends.
- Research and policy papers discussing crime statistics, reporting
  practices, and the challenges of comparing theft data across
  countries and over time.
- Educational explainers on retail shrink, shoplifting measurement,
  and organized retail crime.

No verbatim text, tables, or figures from any external source have been
copied or closely reproduced. All material has been paraphrased,
generalized, and combined with original analysis to respect copyright
and intellectual property requirements.
