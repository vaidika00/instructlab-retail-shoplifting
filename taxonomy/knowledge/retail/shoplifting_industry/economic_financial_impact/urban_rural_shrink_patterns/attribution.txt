Knowledge leaf: Urban vs rural shrink patterns

Purpose:
This knowledge leaf explores typical differences between urban and rural
retail shrink patterns and the factors that drive those differences. It
uses synthetic examples to illustrate how location, store format,
community characteristics, and crime conditions affect shrink levels
and how retailers can tailor loss-prevention strategies by geography.

store situations are illustrative and synthetic; they do not represent
