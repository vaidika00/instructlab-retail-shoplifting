Knowledge leaf: Shrink as % of revenue and profitability impact

Purpose:
This knowledge leaf explains how to calculate retail shrink as a
percentage of revenue and how different shrink levels affect gross
margin and net profitability. It provides synthetic numerical examples
and business scenarios to show the impact of shrink on income
statements and on management decisions about cost control and
investment.
